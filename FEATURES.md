# 🚀 TabSaver 2.1.0: Feature Guide

TabSaver 2.1.0 is a premium session manager designed for productivity. Below is a comprehensive list of all features and exactly how to use them.

---

## 💾 Session Management

### Save Current Window

- **Action**: Saves all open tabs in your current window into a named session.
- **How to access**:
  1. Open the popup.
  2. Type a name in the **"Session Name"** input box.
  3. Click the **Save (Disk)** icon.
- **Keyboard Shortcut**: `Ctrl + Shift + S` (works anywhere in Chrome).

### Restore a Session

- **Action**: Opens all tabs from a saved session at once.
- **How to access**: Click the **Session Title** (blue text) on any session card in the list.

### Selective Restore (Preview)

- **Action**: Pick and choose specific tabs from a session to open instead of the whole group.
- **How to access**:
  1. Click the **Chevron (Down Arrow)** on a session card.
  2. Uncheck any tabs you don't want to open.
  3. Click the **"Open Selected Tabs"** button at the bottom of the list.

### Rename Session

- **Action**: Change the name of an existing session.
- **How to access**: Click the **Pencil icon** on the session card.

### Update Session (Add Tabs)

- **Action**: Add your currently open tabs to an existing session.
- **How to access**: Click the **Plus (+)** icon on the session card.
- **Feature**: It automatically filters out tabs already present in the session.

### Delete & Undo

- **Action**: Remove a session from your list.
- **How to access**: Click the **Trash icon** on the session card.
- **Undo**: A notification (toast) will appear at the bottom for 5 seconds; click **"Undo"** to bring the session back.

### Drag & Drop Reordering

- **Action**: Manually sort your sessions.
- **How to access**: Click and hold the **Drag Handle** (two lines) on the left of any card and drag it to your preferred position.

## 🔍 Organization & Search

### Real-Time Search

- **Action**: Instantly find sessions by name or URL.
- **How to access**: Start typing in the **Search bar** at the top of the popup.
- **Shortcut**: Press `Ctrl + F` while the popup is open to focus the search bar.

### Session Tagging

- **Action**: Categorize your sessions for better organization.
- **How to access**:
  1. Click the **Tag icon** (label) on a session card.
  2. Select a category (Work, Personal, Research, etc.).
- **Removing Tags**: Click the small `×` on any tag displayed on the card.

### Visual Layout Previews

- **Action**: Recognize sessions at a glance.
- **Feature**: Every session automatically includes a visual **Thumbnail** of the tab that was active when you saved it.

### Tab Groups Support

- **Feature**: TabSaver preserves **Chrome Tab Groups**, including their custom titles and colors, so your workspace returns exactly as you left it.

---

## 🧹 Browser Utilities

### Duplicate Tab Cleanup

- **Action**: Closes all duplicate tabs across **all open windows** to save RAM and declutter.
- **How to access**: Click the **Trash/Cleanup icon** in the top toolbar (next to Export/Import).

### Favicon Previews

- **Feature**: Displays the icons of the first four tabs in a session on the card for quick identification.

### Live Sync (Auto-Update)

- **Action**: Automatically updates specific sessions as you browse.
- **How to access**:
  1. Click the **Sync (Arrows)** icon on a session card to enable it (icon will spin).
  2. In **Settings**, ensure "Live sync" is enabled and choose your preferred update interval.
- **Feature**: This allows you to maintain "living" sessions that always reflect your current work.

### Dark Mode

- **Action**: Switch between Light and Dark themes.
- **How to access**: Click the **Sun/Moon icon** in the top-right corner.

### Duplicate Detection (Smart Warning)

- **Action**: Prevents saving redundant data.
- **How to access**: Managed in **Settings**. When enabled, a yellow warning bar appears if the tabs you are about to save are 60% similar to an existing session.

### Storage Monitor

- **Feature**: A visual progress bar appears at the top if you approach the 5MB Chrome local storage limit.

## 📤 Data Portability

### Export Sessions

- **Action**: Backup your data to a file.
- **How to access**: Click the **Up Arrow (Export)** icon in the toolbar. This saves a `.json` file to your computer.

### Import Sessions

- **Action**: Restore data from a backup.
- **How to access**: Click the **Down Arrow (Import)** icon in the toolbar and select your TabSaver `.json` file.
